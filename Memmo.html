Memory Game

Rules of the game:

Click on any two grey boxes. The goal here is to choose pictures that match. If two of the chosen pictures don't match, keep on playing until you get it right. If the pictures match,the two boxes remain open. Do it again and again until you reveal all the pictures! Ones you're done and ready to play again, click "reset" button at the bottom.
